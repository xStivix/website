ABOUT ME

My name is Stefan, I’m a 28-year-old video editor based near Bregenz, Austria. With over five years of experience in the industry, I’m passionate about creating content and exploring the potential of artificial intelligence.

I focus on creating modern, visually appealing content. I mainly work on short-form content like ads, trailers, music videos, image videos, and social media content. I also design and develop websites when needed.

Rather than sticking to only a conventional approach, I strive to stay current with the latest trends.

SERVICES

01

CONCEPTION

Whether it's purely video content, concept development, images or anything else – I adapt to the needs of each project. Depending on its size and scope, I collaborate with other creative professionals to define the right visual direction and lay the groundwork for a smooth and effective production process.

02

POST PRODUCTION

I cover the full post-production workflow – from rough cut to final export. This includes selecting and organising footage, video editing, color grading, sound design, and mixing. I can also add motion graphics, animations, and simple VFX. As well as handle compositing, cleanup, retouching, and template-based animations if needed.

03

AI PRODUCTION

I understand the capabilities and limitations of today’s AI tools – and how to push them to their full potential. I use artificial intelligence to create both realistic and surreal images, videos, and effects, enhancing footage with smart upscaling and frame interpolation. From deepfake applications to lifelike virtual talking models, I’ve developed a workflow that enables fully customizable visuals, tailored precisely to any product or individual.

MY APPROACH

I believe in combining technical excellence with creative direction. Every project begins with understanding your goals and ends with a handcrafted piece. With expertise in both traditional editing and AI technologies, I bring a unique perspective to every project. I use AI as a powerful tool to turn ideas into individual, traditionally edited, AI-enhanced visuals – always refined with a human touch and creative intent.
