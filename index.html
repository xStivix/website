technical specs

My Workflow & Tools

I work with After Effects, Premiere, Photoshop, Topaz Video Enhance, and AI tools like Midjourney, Runway, Kling, and more. My main OS is Windows, but I’m also comfortable working on macOS. I can work with all major camera systems — ARRI Alexa (Log C), RED Komodo & DSMC2 (REDCODE RAW / Log3G10), Blackmagic RAW, film, and all major Sony cameras (S-Log). My colour pipeline is centred around Rec. 709 (Log-to-Rec. 709 conversion), but I'm also comfortable delivering in Rec. 2020 and DCI-P3 workflows if needed. My delivery formats are ProRes 422 HQ, ProRes 4444, or H.264/HEVC (up to 4K).

AI

1. AI Video Production

When working with generative AI for video production, I find that an image to video approach currently offers the maximum amount of creative control. It enables customizable visuals for any product, individual, or creative project. The current workflow with most control usually starts with a source image, AI-generated or photographed, which I shape into a foundation using sketches, 3D models, images, or assets to define the composition. I then apply a style reference and or a tailored prompt to guide the AI toward the desired look, transforming the draft into a high-quality image. Next, I refine the image through editing, color grading, and detail adjustments. Once it aligns with my vision, I input it into an AI image-to-video model with custom prompts, select the best video output, and finalize it with manual refinements.

DIABLO X MIDJOURNEY

This following project is a cinematic, AI-driven unofficial trailer for the game Diablo. It utilizes the latest version of MidJourney (v.7) combined with a straightforward, minimalist AI image-to-video pipeline, requiring only minimal manual retouching. Despite its simplicity, this approach to AI video production can already deliver nice results when complemented by a professional editing process — including custom music mixing, sound and color grading.

2. AI Image Production

Creating high-quality and coherent videos—whether traditionally or with AI—still demands alot of technical expertise, experience, time, and a clear creative vision. On the other hand AI image generation is now more accessible than ever. Professional and compelling results still benefit from a careful guidance and refinement, but often all it takes is just a well-crafted prompt, and fully customizable outcomes are achievable. I created these images to showcase AI’s potential in visual production, illustrating how it can support or even transform workflows as an innovative, efficient tool for advertising, social media, and branding.
