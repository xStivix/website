AI WORKFLOW

Generative AI

When working with AI in video production, generative AI is by far the most transformative innovation, fundamentally changing the way we create content. I find that an image-to-video approach currently offers the highest level of creative control. Starting from a base image, AI-generated or photographed, I define the composition using sketches, 3D models, or images. I guide the AI with style references and tailored prompts to generate a high-quality image, which I refine through editing and color grading. This image then serves as the input for an AI image-to-video model, where I select the best result and finalize it with manual adjustments.

Production Breakdown

This is an early proof of concept built entirely from AI-generated footage. Created in late 2024 using an image-to-video workflow, similar to today’s best practices showen above but less efficient, getting usable results still required extensive manual retouching and classic post-production. This clip is the result of over 80 hours of hands-on work: editing, sound design, VFX, color grading, and prompt engineering. Images were created with MidJourney 6.1, refined in Photoshop and Magnific AI, animated with Kling 1.6 AI and Veo2, and finalized in After Effects with flares, flames, lighting effects, camera shake, and a 3D Porsche logo.Final polish and upscaling were done with Topaz AI.
