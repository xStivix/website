ABOUT ME

My Name is Stefan, I'm 28 years old, based in Bregenz, Austria. My passion lies at the intersection of traditional video editing and artificial intelligence.

I create visually appealing content that pushes boundaries while maintaining cinematic quality. I specialize in short-form content such as advertisements, trailers, music videos, image videos, and social media content.

Rather than sticking to a conventional approach, I strive to stay current with the latest trends—merging technological advance with artistic vision.
